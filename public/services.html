Our Services

Comprehensive moving solutions for residential and commercial customers.

Local Moving

Moving within Springfield or surrounding areas within 50 miles? Our local moving service is fast, efficient, and affordable. We provide the truck, the crew, and the equipment. You provide the destination. We handle the rest with care and precision.

  • 2-hour minimum, no hidden fees
  • Fully equipped trucks with padding and straps
  • Senior and military discounts
  • Same-week availability

Long Distance

Relocating to another state or coast to coast? American Horizon coordinates every detail of your long-distance move. Our drivers are experienced, our trucks are maintained to the highest standard, and your belongings are fully insured from pickup to delivery.

  • Real-time shipment tracking
  • Full valuation coverage available
  • Consolidated and dedicated options
  • 50-state authority and insurance

Packing Services

Let our trained packing teams handle the hard work. We use premium packing materials, industrial-grade bubble wrap, and custom crating for fragile and high-value items. Every box is labeled by room and contents for easy unpacking at your new home.

  • Full or partial packing options
  • Eco-friendly materials available
  • Fine art and antiques specialists
  • Unpacking and setup included

Commercial Moving

Minimize downtime with our corporate relocation services. We work around your schedule, including weekends and after-hours. Our commercial team handles office furniture, IT equipment, filing systems, and sensitive documents with speed and discretion.

  • After-hours and weekend moves
  • IT disconnect and reconnect
  • Asset inventory management
  • Minimal business interruption

Storage Solutions

Our climate-controlled storage facility is clean, secure, and monitored 24/7. Whether you need short-term storage between moves or long-term preservation, we offer flexible month-to-month terms with easy access scheduling via our online portal.

  • Climate-controlled units
  • 24/7 video monitoring
  • Month-to-month lease terms
  • Online access scheduling

White Glove Service

Our premium white glove service includes full packing, disassembly and reassembly of furniture, careful wrapping of every item, and complete unpacking at the destination. We place everything exactly where you want it and remove all packing debris.

  • Full-service concierge moving
  • Furniture disassembly and assembly
  • Custom crating for valuables
  • Packing debris removal
