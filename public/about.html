About Our Company

A moving company built on old-fashioned values and modern standards.

Our Story

American Horizon Moving was founded in Springfield, Illinois in 2012. The founders, Mike Corrigan and Dave Henshaw, had spent fifteen years working for national moving chains. They saw the same problems over and over late trucks, damaged items, and fine print that let companies off the hook.

They started American Horizon with one truck, a warehouse lease on Industrial Boulevard, and a simple promise: show up on time, take care of the customers belongings like they were your own, and charge a fair price. That promise became the foundation of everything we do.

Today we operate a fleet of 22 trucks, employ over 60 people, and serve customers in all 50 states. But the core values have not changed. Every move is staffed by American Horizon employees. Every truck is owned and maintained by us. Every customer gets the same direct accountability that we started with in 2012.
